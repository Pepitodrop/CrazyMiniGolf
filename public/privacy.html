Privacy notice

Crazy Mini Golf does not use analytics, advertising trackers, user accounts, third-party fonts, or marketing cookies.

Local game data

The game stores progress, local scores, medals, and style points only in your browser's Local Storage. This data remains on your device and can be deleted with the in-game reset button or your browser settings.

Hosting data

The hosting provider may process technical request data such as IP address, timestamp, requested file, user agent, and response status to deliver the website and protect its infrastructure. The exact retention and processing rules depend on the hosting provider used for the deployment.

Network requests

The application itself has no application backend and does not transmit scores or gameplay telemetry. The Content Security Policy restricts connections to the same origin.
